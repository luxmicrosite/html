Work 3 — Braille on Film

Work details

  • Title: the owl’s daughter
  • Year: 2023

Image description

A 16mm film projector sits on a stand, by the window giving onto the garden. Across the entire film that has been loaded on the projector, my father has written the short story of ‘the owl’s daughter’ in Farsi braille.

This story is a fictionalised account of a journey learning to read in one’s mother tongue, which you can hear with his voice in Minevissam, screening in the blackbox space.

Tactile: You are welcome to touch or read the braille on the 16 mm film, whilst the owl, carved out of dark blue ink on the window, watches over you. Ask our Front of House staff for assistance.
