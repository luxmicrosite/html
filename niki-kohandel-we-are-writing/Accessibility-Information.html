Accessibility Information

Support for visitors

  • Screenings with Audio Description take place daily. Please check the schedule below.
  • Audio Guide for the space orientation and exhibition layout can be accessed via Navilens QR code in the reception or ask the Front of House staff for assistance
  • Image descriptions of the visual works are avaialable via Navilens QR code placed near the artworks
  • Hearing loops are available.
  • Large print guides and magnifying glasses are available.
  • Plain English and Audio version of the exhibition text is available.

Building access

  • Our building is step-free.
  • Accessible toilets are available.
