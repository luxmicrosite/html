Accessibility Information

Support for visitors with visual and hearing needs

  • The film includes Audio Description — please ask Front of House staff for assistance.
  • Hearing loops are available.
  • Large print guides and magnifying glasses are available.

Building access

  • Our building is step-free.
  • Accessible toilets are available.

Screening Schedule

The film is screened in 20-minute slots during the opening hours. Screenings with Audio Description are marked on the schedule.

  • 6:00 – 6:40 pm
  • 6:20 – 6:40 pm (with Audio Description)
  • 6:40 – 7:00 pm
  • 7:00 – 7:20 pm (with Audio Description)
  • 7:20 – 7:40 pm
  • 7:40 – 8:00 pm (with Audio Description)
